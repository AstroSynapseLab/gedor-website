Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your email address. That is the only personal data we store.

Why we collect it

Your email is used exclusively to notify you when early access opens and to send occasional product updates related to Gedor.ai. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond direct product communication.

What we do not do

  • We do not track you across other websites.
  • We do not run analytics or behavioural profiling.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your email for marketing unrelated to Gedor.ai.

Where your data is stored

Your email is processed via Formspree and stored on EU-based infrastructure. No data leaves the EU.

Your rights

You can withdraw consent and request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@gedor.ai. We will remove your data within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at privacy@gedor.ai.
